HEMP FOR HAITI

Seeking to benefit humanity and earth
by creating handmade hemp goods
with a purpose.

Hemp for Haiti is a business started by Leah Jaicks in May of 2017, specializing in handmade hemp jewelry, accessories, decor, and more! A quarter of all sales are donated to High Hopes for Haiti - the Mortel Foundation to help fund education for students in St. Marc, Haiti.
